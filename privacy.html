Privacy & Security

Last Updated: April 2026

Our Commitment

At FashionHub, your trust is our most valuable accessory. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring that your shopping experience is safe and secure.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide a personalized style experience, including:

  • Contact details (Name, Email, Phone Number).
  • Shipping and billing addresses.
  • Purchase history and saved "Wishlist" items.

2. Data Security

We use SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption to protect your data during checkout. Your payment information is processed through secure gateways and is never stored on our local servers.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and keep items in your cart. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function correctly.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you or ask for your account to be deleted at any time through your Profile dashboard.
